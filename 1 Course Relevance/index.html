Engineering Design and Innovation

Course Objectives:

1. To develop problem solving ability using programming skills by exploring and proposing solutions to realistic/social problems.
2. To Evaluate alternative approaches, and justify the use of selected tools and methods.
3. To emphasize learning activities those are long-term, inter-disciplinary and student-centric.
4. To engage students in rich and authentic learning experiences.
5. To provide every student the opportunity to get involved either individually or as a group so as to develop team skills and learn professionalism.
6. To develop an ecosystem to promote entrepreneurship and research culture among the students

Course Relevance:

Project Centric Learning (PCL) is a powerful tool for students to work in areas of their choice and strengths. Along with course based projects, curriculum can be enriched with semester long Engineering Design and Development courses, in which students can solve socially relevant problems using various technologies from relevant disciplines. The various socially relevant domains can be like Health care, Agriculture, Defense, Education, Smart City, Smart Energy and Swaccha Bharat Abhiyan.

To gain the necessary skills to tackle such projects, students can select relevant online courses and acquire skills from numerous sources under guidance of faculty and enrich their knowledge in the project domain, thereby achieving project centric learning. Modern world sustained and advanced through the successful completion of projects. In short, if students are prepared for success in life, we need to prepare them for a project-based world. It is a style of active learning and inquiry-based learning.

Course Relevance:

Software project development comes under the category of project based learning (PBL). PBL is an instructional approach designed to give students the opportunity to develop knowledge and skills through engaging projects set around challenges and problems they may face in the real world by using domain specific language technologies. PBL is "learning by doing."

Modern world sustained and advanced through the successful completion of projects. In short, if students are prepared for success in life, we need to prepare them for a project-based world. It is a style of active learning and inquiry-based learning. Project based learning will also redefine the role of teacher as mentor in the learning process.

Design and Thinkink

Course Objectives:

1.To reflect on the design thinking process used in the previous semester project and evaluate its effectiveness in achieving the project goals.
2.To identify potential areas for improvement in the design thinking process and propose alternative approaches.
3.To deepen understanding of design thinking principles and how they can be applied in real-world projects.
4.To practice research and analytical skills by gathering and synthesizing data from various sources.
5.To enhance communication and presentation skills by conveying research findings in a clear and compelling manner.

Course Relevance

This research paper can serve as a capstone project for a design thinking course, providing an opportunity for students to apply and demonstrate their understanding of key concepts and principles.It can also help students to develop critical thinking skills by evaluating the strengths and weaknesses of their previous project and identifying areas for improvement.

By conducting research and analyzing data, students can deepen their understanding of design thinking and its application in real-world contexts.The paper can help students to hone their writing and presentation skills, which are important in many academic and professional settings.
